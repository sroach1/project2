THE BUGS




Many of the mosquitoes in Florida transmit diseases to humans and other animals. Symptoms of these illnesses can be mild or very severe.

Some of these diseases are chikungunya fever, dengue fever, malaria, yellow fever, and Rift Valley fever.

Florida is ranked number one as the state with the worst bug problems.

Other popular bugs in Florida are the brown recluse spiders, cockroaches, bed bugs and fire ants.
